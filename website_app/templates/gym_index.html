WELCOME TO VELVET ROYAL GYM

Welcome to Velvet Royal Gym, where your fitness journey begins and excelling becomes second nature.

VELVET ROYAL GYM provides a 24/7 Fitness facility to residents of BELAGAUM as well as surrounding areas to help people reach and maintain their fitness goals. We combine different types of latest fitness equipment to meet different fitness needs and levels. At VELVET ROYAL GYM you’ll find all the latest strength and cardio equipment along with an energetic group exercise program that includes Heavy weight, fitness, Personal training, Muscle Building and many other cardio classes. You’ll find a supportive environment with all kinds of people who are working just as hard as you to meet their goals.

Our trainer is committed to offer our members a great fitness experience. We can ensure that we will help you to build a proper body shape, according your preference.

HOW WILL WE HELP YOU ?

Regular physical activity is one of the most important things you can do for your health. Being physically active can improve your mental health, reduce the risk of getting affected by a disease, build strength, and improve your ability to do day to day activities. By joining a gym you can increase your confidence, build a strong mindset, refresh your mind and be a better version of yourself.
